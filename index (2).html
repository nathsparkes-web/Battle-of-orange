Procedure

Opening of the Court

There is no prosecution or defense counsel in these proceedings. Spenny presents each case in turn, using his own footage as the primary evidence. Moran presides. All other members of the tour sit as jury — including the four individuals facing charges. A defendant does not vote on their own charge, but remains part of the jury for every other case, including those of their fellow defendants.
Narrator — Spenny

This court is convened to establish what took place last night during the events that have since been referred to as the Battle of the Balcony. Four members of this tour face charges arising from that evening. I was present throughout, camera in hand, and the footage I gathered forms the basis of the evidence you are about to hear.

Each case will be presented in turn. Each defendant will have the opportunity to respond. The jury will then vote on each charge individually. I would ask that the proceedings be given the attention they deserve.

Judge — Moran

This court is now in session. Four individuals stand accused, across seven separate charges. Each will be heard fairly and in full before any verdict is reached. Members of the jury, you are reminded that you may not vote on a charge brought against yourselves, but you are expected to participate fully in every other case before this court. Let us begin.
